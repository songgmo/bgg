About
Busan National Gugak Center

Busan National Gugak Center was established on October 28 2008 in order to preserve and promote the traditional performing arts of Busan․Yeongnam region. It aims to contribute in developing arts and culture by creating traditions with local sentiment.
The Center, on the basis of Busan as a center place in Asia Pacific region, also plans to raise the dignity of the city and to spread Korean traditional culture to the world by designing new shows and concerts.

Gugak Performance Groups

Traditional Music Orchestra/
Traditional Vocal Music Group

Traditional Music Orchestra and Traditional Vocal Music Group consisted of more than fifty artists who are the best musicians with great artistic ability. The director as well as the members endeavor to continue Korea’s tradition to the future by performing all kinds of music, including old elegant music, folk music, and creative music. Especially, the group puts effort to preserve and to transmit uniqueness of Yeongnam arts by making high quality performance, so that the modern audience understands it. At the same time, the group tries that the music survives and stays around the people’s life, not only in Korea but also in all the global society.

Dance Theater

Including the director and choreographers, the thirty members in Dance Theater are the best artists in Korea with great ability. They perform all types of Korean dance, from court festive dance to folk and creative dances, in order to transmit and to develop the traditional dance heritage. Moreover, it tries to introduce and to promote Yeongnam dances which are characterized as being active and stylish. With the bodily movements, the dancers are now making performances that can communicate with audience.
