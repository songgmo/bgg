Reservation

The story of Island full of life styles and sentiments of local people

The story unfold conflicts and love among the family members of Deuksim grandma who can not help but stays in the island. The musical drama composed of 14 scenes will invite you to the artistic world of southern folk music.

Program

Scene 1 The summer in the Seaweed Island (Present) Scene 2 Let go up to the land (Present, Grandma’s House) Scene 3 Ganggangsullae under the moonlight (In 1943, Mokpo) Scene 4 For him, my heart beats like Dung-deong-ae (In 1944, hill at the back) Scene 5 The summer in the Seaweed Island (Present) Scene 6 The Soul, oh the soul (In 1954) Scene 7 Fools worse than the Seaweed (In 1954) Scene 8 Let’s work and farm (1960~70s) Scene 9 It is the spring time... (In 1980, village market) Scene 10 Let’s live like river and the wind (In 1983) Scene 11 Even if the cloud hides the flowing time (Present) Scene 12 Though the wave and wind swept away (In 1986) Scene 13 The breezy wind under bright moon light (Present) Scene 14 Like the seaweed dried over night (Present)
