The tasty words and witty actions of a humorous performing troupe 'The Gwangdae'!

The concert presents audience an energetic traditional theatric gala performance for the holiday. All the families or friends and lovers can enjoy the joyful moment together with humor and eye catching movements.

Program

1. Pangut (standing samulnori) 2. Ttangjulnori 3. Sangsoenori, Seoljanggunori 4. Sajanoleum 5. Sogonori 6. Beonanori 7. Jukbangulnori 8. Yeoldubal sangmonori
