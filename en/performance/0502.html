Our dance and our music under the autumn moonlight~

A rich and cheerful concert for the productive harvesting season. The concert is designed to share happiness and joy with the audience, along with the good energy comes from the bright full moon. Just like lots of fruits and foods on the table, our show presents the audience a variety of music and dances.
