Close your eyes and feel the music! Realistic immersive sound effect for the true beauty of upper class music!

The program contains the best of both string and wind ensemble music, such as Byeolgok and Gwanakyeongsanhoesang. In order to deliver the deep and true sound that the best musicians of Busan National Gugak Center produce, the stage adopted realistic sound effect system. The audience can hear the music coming from the speaker equipped up and down, left and right, as well as front and back. Please enjoy the essence of jeongak music with the best sound and the best system.

Program

1. String Ensemble_‘Byeolgok’ - Dodeuri <Suyeonjangjigok, Songguyeojigok> - Sanghyeondodeuri(Seventh movement) - Hahyeondodeuri - Yeombuldodeuri - Taryeong - Gunak - Cheonnyeonmanse <Gyemyeongarakdodeuri, Yangcheongdodeuri, Ujogarakdodeuri> 2. Wind ensemble_‘Yeongsanhoesang’ - Sangryeongsan - Jungryeongsan - Seryeongsan - Garakdeori - Samhyeondodeuri - Yeombuldodeuri - Taryeong - Gunak
