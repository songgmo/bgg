The last days of traditional arts, let’s remember what had happened to the artists!

The performance is a multi artistic mega show featuring regional culture in Dongnae, Busan. In early spring 1946, a boy was fascinated by a photo showing Dongnae crane dance and decided to find the master. The story tells his journey to train the dance and become a beautiful dancer.
