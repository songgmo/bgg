How to Receive Tickets

Tickets available at the ticket box on the day at the concert venue pick up availbale from 1 hour before the performance begins. Please bring the relevant document for discount.

Unless prepared, the rest of the ticket price must be paid back.

Cancellation Policy

Cancel or change available only until 6 pm one day prior to the event.

It is unavailable to cancel or change the ticket on the day of the event.

Seat Share Project

Seat sharing is a donation program providing some seats for people in culturally disadvantaged class. In order to provide opportunity to enjoy arts and leisure for more people so to reduce the cultural gap in the community, Busan National Gugak Center runs the sponsorship sharing 10% of the seats. Please pay your attention and participate in our program.
