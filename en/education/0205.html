More fun and better time with special lecturers!

This program combines lecture in humanities and the concerts, and thus the audience not only gains knowledge on culture but also enjoy the performances. The lecturer tells about the history and background of the performance the audience are seeing in understandable level. As feeling and learning about the arts and culture, the audience who are busy and exhaust in modern society will have a healing time.

Program

January Talk The lecture is related to the concert <It is GOOD!> and the representative of a performance company An Dae-cheon of ‘The Gwangdae’ gives a talk on traditional theatric performance and its new creation. March Talk The lecture is related to 2023 Traditional Music Orchestra regular concert, <The charm and style of jeongak>. Song Ji-won, the representative of Music Humanities Research Center who is hosting <Gugak sanchaek> in Gugak FM tells about the royal ceremonies and music during the Joseon Dynasty. May Talk The lecture is related to the barrier free musical <Ogi>, and Kim Ji-won who directed the program gives talk on the theme ‘there is no barrier free.’

* Schedule and education are subject to change.
