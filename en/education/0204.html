The concert for culturally disadvantaged people

In order to help our neighbors who have difficulties in coming to the concert veneu, the musicians visit the audience to communicate with and show them music. It is a warm and interactive time with Korean traditional music. Any facilities or agency for disabled, seniors, and patient nursing located in Busan can apply for the program year around. Students in Busan, Gyeongbuk, Yeongnam Universities participate in the concert as performers, and bringing beautiful melody for weak and disadvantaged people in our society.
