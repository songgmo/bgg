Teaching college of education students a pedagogical approach for traditional music

The program is designed to provide pedagogical tips and methods to the students who are now enrolled in the college of education and will be a teacher in the future. The gugak specialists train the participants how to teach theory, listening, and practicing instruments based on the themes in the music text books. On the last day of program, the participants see the concert related to the music listed in the textbook so that they can connect what they have learnt to the actual performance.

Program

Theory, listening, singing, playing instruments
