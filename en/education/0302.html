Opportunity for the youth to practice gugak in Busan National Gugak Center

This educational program includes not only the learning and practice, but also seeing the performance. Linking the classwork to the performance, the program aims to raise students’ interest on gugak. From the second half year in 2023, the program adds another activity to see realistic contents and digital reading room at the Education Experience Center.

Program

A Type: (3-4 grade) Daechwita, Cheoyong dance, Folksong(practice), Handy fan dance, Pangut B Type: (5 grade and over) Cheonnyeonmanse, Gayageum singing, Pansori(practice), Ganggangsullae, Pangut * Including 20 minute safety education, the program is subject to change.

* Schedule and education are subject to change.
