A culture day with gugak specialists

On the culture day, the last Wednesday in each month, a gugak specialist gives a talk and introduces you music. New specialist comes for each time, and shares the story of their life and arts with the audience. Time to time, the audience might have a short sing-along time. The audience who are busy and tired with their work and life will be relieved with honest story full of hope and warm regards.
