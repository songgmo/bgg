Let’s experience the music and see the concert! Gugak One-day class.

The program provides an opportunity to experience gugak, such as learning samulnori, dance, and folksongs, or seeing special concerts, especially to the groups who visit Busan National Gugak Center. From the second half year in 2023, the program adds another activity to see realistic contents and digital reading room at the Education Experience Center, before or after the activity.

Program

Gugak practice (samulnori, folksong, dance, etc.), see concert
