The teachers completely absorbed in traditional music in kid’s eye level.

In relation to education training center of Busan metropolitan education office, the Center provides program training how to teach traditional music listed in the music textbook. The participating teachers not only learning pedagogical methodology from experienced master musicians, but also see the concerts in Busan National Gugak Center. The program is known to be highly helpful that it improves the teaching skills combining the theory and the music together.

Program

Introduction to gugak theory and listening, Teaching and learning songs for elementary level, Teaching instruments for elementary students, Concerts
