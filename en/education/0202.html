Let’s learn Yeongnam dance from the masters!

The program provides opportunity to learn various dances transmitting in Yeongnam regions, such as Dongnae crane dance, and Jinju sword dance. Not only the joyful and energetic regional dances, the students can also learn court festive dance which is rarely taught in a local area. The holder and practitioner of Inangible Cultural Property teaches the true beauty of the dance to the students. Within the session, the students can see many other types of dances as Busan National Gugak Center plans to show the special concert.

Program

Basic movements for traditional dance, Dongrae crane dance, Jinju sword dance, court festive dance
